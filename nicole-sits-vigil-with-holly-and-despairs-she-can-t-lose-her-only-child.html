Nicole Sits Vigil With Holly And Despairs-She Cant Lose Her Only Child!

Days of Our Lives spoilers and updates tease Nicole DiMera (Arianne Zucker) will sit vigil with Holly Jonas (Ashley Puzemis) who’s in a coma and on a ventilator. Nicole despairs, knowing Holly could be on that life support machine forever-she can’t lose her only child!

Days Of Our Lives Spoilers: Minimum Brain Function

Holly had overdosed on some pills that were likely laced with fentanyl, causing her to go into seizures nearly immediately. Nicole doesn’t even know where she got the pills, she had told Tate Black (Jamie Martin Mann) she got them from someone at school.

Two other teenagers had died from overdosing likely on the same substance, from what Rafe Hernandez (Galen Gering) said. Tripp Johnson (Lucas Adams) said Holly was stable but had minimum brain function-hence her being put on life support; Nicole can’t believe this is happening, she’s in shock.

They just have to take it day by day, not knowing if Holly’s brain functions will ever heal or if she’ll ever be the same again. Nicole is beyond afraid that she’ll have to eventually make a decision to let her daughter go.

DOOL Spoilers: Nicole DiMera Can’t Believe She Didn’t Know

While EJ DiMera (Dan Feurreriegel) swears his stepdaughter doesn’t use drugs, Nicole can’t believe she didn’t know-even though she knows she experimented drinking.

They’d came home on Christmas Day to find Holly having one heck of a hangover-Nicole blames herself for leaving Holly alone on her birthday.

Even though Johnny DiMera (Carson Boatman) kept her company part of the time, he needed to go support Chanel Dupree (Raven Bowens) when Paulina Price (Jackée Harry) went to the hospital.

Nicole beats herself up inwardly for not being a better mother to Holly, being involved in her own life and loves; Holly probably felt like she didn’t exist in her eyes.

Days Of Our Lives Spoilers: Nicole DiMera Prays, And Prays Hard

All Nicole can do is pray, and pray hard for Holly to heal and recover, although the odds right now are pretty slim and EJ’s railroading Tate. EJ is lashing out because he can’t do anything but lash out, the situation seems so hopeless he feels helpless-just like Nicole herself does.

Nicole keeps beating herself up inside for focusing on anything and everything besides her daughter these past few years, she lost precious time with Holly.

She won’t be able to ever forgive herself if Holly doesn’t make it-and she will put her on a leash if she does. Nicole will never let her daughter out of her sight again if she’ll only wake up and be herself again.
