Kristin Davis News - Us Weekly

Name:Kristin Davis

Hometown:Boulder, CO

Birthday:February 24, 1965

About

Born in Boulder, Colo., in 1965, Kristin Davis is part of he now-legendary cast of Sex and the City as sweetly optimistic Charlotte York.

She achieved widespread fame in her infamous role as Brooke on Melrose Place, and, after she as written off, she moved on to guest appearances on hit shows such as Friends. She also appeared on the silver screen in films including The Shaggy Dog.

A recovering alcoholic, the actress has been romantically involved with leading Hollywood men including Matthew Perry, Alec Baldwin, and Liev Schreiber, but she remains tightlipped about her love life.
