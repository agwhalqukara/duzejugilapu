Allison Williams Shares Photo From &quot;Heavenly&quot; Honeymoon

Allison Williams had a perfect-looking wedding, so it’s only natural that her honeymoon with new hubby Ricky Van Veen was equally as idyllic.

“Honeymoon was Heavenly & I’m Happy to be Home,” the Girls star, 27, captioned a gorgeous Instagram photo of herself and the magical-looking nighttime view of what appears to be Positano, Italy, on Sunday, Oct. 4.

Williams married businessman Van Veen, 34, on Sept. 19 in a star-studded ranch wedding. Brian Williams’ daughter donned an exquisite, handmade couture Oscar de la Renta gown.

As previously reported by Us, Tom Hanks who spent about $35 to become ordained, officiated the duo’s wedding. Hanks’ wife Rita, who plays Williams’ mom on the hit HBO show, was also on hand to witness their vows along with Katy Perry, John Mayer, Bruce Springsteen, Andy Cohen, Lena Dunham, and more celebrity pals. 

Van Veen and Williams dated for three years before announcing their engagement in February.
