Cara Delevingne and Kate Moss Model for My Burberry Fragrance Campaign

Joining forces!

Kate Moss and Cara Delevingne — two of the UK’s biggest, wildest supermodels from different generations — team up for the first time in the stunning new campaign for My Burberry fragrance. Shot by renowned photographer Mario Testino, the two blonde beauties pose together wearing the British label’s Sandringham trench coat — and little else.

“For me it was so amazing to work with Kate, this was my first time,” Delevingne, 22, told Vogue. “Although I’ve known her for a while I’ve never seen her at work. On set she is just amazing and even moor magnetic, everybody watched her.”

Her role model model Moss, 40, echoed Delevingne’s enthusiasm over the shoot. “I was so excited when I heard I would be shooting with Cara — I had met her a few times, but never worked with her, so I knew we were going to have a brilliant time, and have a real laugh on set,” Moss told Vogue. “It was such a simple and natural shoot; it was just Cara, me and our trench coats, and it meant we were really able to just relax and have fun on the day — it was a perfect day.”
