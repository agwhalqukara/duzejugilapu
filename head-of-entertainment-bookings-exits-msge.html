Head Of Entertainment Bookings Exits MSGE

LOS ANGELES (VIP NEWS) — Dana DuFine has left her job at MSG Entertainment where she was senior vp/head of West Coast operations.


DuFine joined MSGE in 2014 and was based in Los Angeles. The executive`s portfolio included top-grossing New York City venue Madison Square Garden and L.A.`s The Forum.


Named to Billboard`s Women in Music list for 2015, she was credited with creating the company`s cross-venue touring division, leveraging the booking power of MSGE`s buildings in major cities.


MSGE, a partnership between the Madison Square Garden Company and Irving Azoff, was announced in September, 2013.


DuFine had previously held positions at Azoff management arm Front Line Entertainment and Immortal Records/Virgin EMI. She exited the MSGE position earlier this month.

–According to Billboard
