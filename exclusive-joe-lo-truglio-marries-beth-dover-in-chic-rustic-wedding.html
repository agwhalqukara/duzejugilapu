Exclusive: Joe Lo Truglio Marries Beth Dover in Chic, Rustic Wedding

Joe Lo Truglio is officially a taken man! The Brooklyn Nine-Nine actor, 43, married Beth Dover in a chic, rustic wedding in Santa Barbara on April 19, Us Weekly confirms.

The couple, who have shared the screen together on Burning Love in addition to the hit Fox comedy, made it official at the rustic Santa Margarita Ranch in San Luis Obispo, Calif., in front of over 200 friends and family members. Included in those friends and family members were co-stars Paul Rudd, Andy Samberg, Bobby Cannavale, David Wain, Chelsea Perretti, Jordan Peele, Ken and Erica Marino, Thomas Lennon, and Kerri Kenney-Silver.
