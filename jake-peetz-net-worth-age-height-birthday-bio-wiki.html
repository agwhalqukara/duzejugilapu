Jake Peetz Biography

Jake Peetz is one of the most popular and richest Athlete who was born on April 5, 1983 in O'Neill, Nebraska, United States.

On January 31, 2020, Peetz was named the quarterbacks coach of the Carolina Panthers under new head coach Matt Rhule.

After spending a year at Alabama, on February 20, 2019, Peetz was named the running backs coach of the Carolina Panthers after former coach Jim Skipper retired. The Panthers went 5-11 and head coach Ron Rivera was fired heading into Week 14. Under his coaching, Christian McCaffrey led the NFL in touches, total touchdowns and all-purpose yards. He also led all running backs in points scored and receptions. He was named 1st-team All-Pro & was named to the Pro Bowl.
