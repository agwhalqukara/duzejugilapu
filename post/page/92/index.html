Cicely Tyson, iconic US Black actress, dies at 96

published on August 17, 2024
Actress Cicely Tyson, who specialised in portraying strong Black women caught up in life’s struggles during a 60-year career that earned her three Emmy Awards and a Tony Award, died on Thursday at age 96, her manager said in a statement. No cause of death was given. Tyson had recently completed a memoir, Just As I Am, which was released just this week.
