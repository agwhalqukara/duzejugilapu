PICS AND GRAPHIC VIDEO: Vehicle Drives Into Innocent Bystanders At 1824 Club

A vehicle lost control and drove into innocent bystanders outside the 1824 club in Langata today at around 3 AM.

Around four people were seriously injured and this site is yet to verify reports that one person might have lost his life in the incident

Outside 1824 has always been an accident in waiting. Drunk people always stand on the road thinking they own the road. Cars are carelessly parked and something must be urgently done.

The vehicle, a Prado #KBC 822W lost control as alleged by a witness
