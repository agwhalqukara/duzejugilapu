Paul Walker Best Man in Brother's Wedding 6 Weeks Before Death: Photos

Fond family memories. Just six weeks before he tragically died in a car accident, Paul Walker reportedly served as best man at his brother Caleb’s wedding to girlfriend Stephanie on Oct. 19. Us Weekly has obtained photos of the Walker family celebrating the wedding day at Dove Canyon Country Club In Rancho Santa Margarita, Calif.

In one touching snapshot, Paul, 40, and Caleb, 36, posed with their arms around each other and smiles on their faces. The blonde siblings looked handsome in three-piece grey suits for the big day. According to the New York Daily News, Paul acted as his brother’s best man.

In another photo from the wedding, the Fast and the Furious star posed in a group photo with the 14-member wedding party, which also included his younger brother Cody, 25. Paul and Cody were photographed sharing a laugh at the reception while giving a speech about their brother.

Just six weeks later, Paul died in a car crash on Saturday, Nov. 30 after attending a charity event. He is survived by father Paul Walker III, mother Cheryl Walker, brothers Cody and Caleb, sister Ashley and 15-year-old daughter Meadow with ex-girlfriend Rebecca McBrain.

One day after his son’s death, Paul Walker III broke down in tears while speaking about his son with CBS2. “I’m just glad that every time I saw him, I told him I loved him,” Walker Sr. said while crying. “And he would say the same thing to me.”
