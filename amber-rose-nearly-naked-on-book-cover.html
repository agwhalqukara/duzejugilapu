Amber Rose Nearly Naked on Book Cover

Shocking! Amber Rose shared the shocking new cover of her book, How to Be a Bad Bitch, on Saturday, June 6, and it features the social media-savvy model nearly naked.

"Check out the Official cover for my book," Wiz Khalifa's ex wrote on her Instagram. "How To Be A Bad Bitch Shot by David LaChapelle and make sure you preorder your copy now on Amazon."

In the shocking cover image, Rose wears a skimpy, barely there string bikini that covers part of her boobs. The mom of one poses from the side atop jagged rocks with a bow and arrow in hand. A naked man resembling a Greek or Roman warrior, meanwhile, is dead from multiple arrow wounds on the rocks below her.

Rose's latest racy social media share was posted several months after she slammed slut shamers for their negativity. In fact, the model told critics: "I feed off that s—."
