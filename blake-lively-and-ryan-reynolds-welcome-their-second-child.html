Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds Welcome Their Second Child

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds have reportedly welcomed their second child.

According to E! News, the baby was born in New York at a Manhattan hospital. However, no further details — including the baby’s sex and name — are known.

“They are so excited,” – an insider told E! News

Another source close to the family revealed to the publication that Taylor Swift was headed to the hospital to visit the newborn on the morning of Sept. 30. Guys, the baby might just be the youngest member of Taylor’s squad!

Blake made her baby bump debut at the 2016 Cannes Film Festival in May, when she stepped out for the premiere of her film Café Society.

Furthermore, last month Lively celebrated their little one’s upcoming arrival with an intimate baby shower, which was held at the Bedford Post Inn in New York.

The actors are already parents to daughter James, who was born in December 2014

Both Reynolds and Lively come from big families and have previously revealed their hopes for a big family of their own.

“I’m one of five kids. My husband is one of four, so we’re officially breeders,” – joked Lively.
