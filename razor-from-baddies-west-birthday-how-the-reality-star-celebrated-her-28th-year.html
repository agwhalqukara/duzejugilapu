Razor from Baddies West Birthday: How the Reality Star Celebrated Her 28th Year

Razor’s Zodiac Sign and Personality

However, Geminis are also friendly, witty, and adaptable. They are good at communicating and expressing themselves. They are curious and eager to learn new things. They are also fun-loving and adventurous, always ready for a challenge.

Razor’s personality reflects her zodiac sign. She is a natural-born leader who is energetic and charismatic. She is also selfless and willing to help others, without expecting anything in return. Razor from Baddies West birthday was a memorable and fun-filled event. The reality star showed her fans and followers how to live life to the fullest and enjoy every moment.
